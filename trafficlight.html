Traffic lights controlling - how does it work nowadays?

Traffic controllers use the concept of phases, which are directions of movement grouped together. For instance, a simple T-junction may have three vechicle movement phases, one for each arm of the junction, There may be additional phases for other movements such as pedestrians, cyclists, bus lanes of tramways
